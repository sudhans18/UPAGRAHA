GAME OF CODES

Where Logic Meets Strategy

Game of Codes is a high-energy technical competition that blends logical reasoning, circuit problem-solving, and strategic gameplay. Participants must think quickly, coordinate as a team, and adapt under pressure as technical challenges are influenced by real-time game actions.

Round I: CROSSWORD CONQUEST

In this round, teams take on a timed technical crossword designed to test conceptual understanding and technical vocabulary. Success depends on logical thinking, speed, and effective teamwork.

Round 1 Rules

  • Teams must complete a timed technical crossword.
  • Evaluation is based on accuracy, number of correct entries, and completion time.
  • Crossword difficulty increases progressively.
  • Top-performing teams advance to Round 2.

Round II: UNO-CONTROLLED CIRCUIT CHALLENGE

This round combines hands-on circuit problem-solving with a live UNO game running in parallel. Gameplay directly affects the technical task, adding an element of unpredictability.

Round 2 Format

Task Solver

Works on a circuit-based problem using simulation tools.

UNO Player

Competes in a live UNO game.

UNO cards trigger penalties or advantages such as freezes, key restrictions, role swaps, and temporary immunity.

The first team to complete the technical task wins.
If no team completes the task, results are decided based on maximum progress with minimum penalties.

EVENT RULES

  • Each team must consist of exactly 2 members.
  • Teams must strictly follow the given format and time limits.
  • Any rule violation may lead to disqualification.
  • The judges’ decision is final.
