⚔ GAME OF CODES ⚔

Step into a high-energy fusion of logic, electronics, and strategy with “GAME OF CODES”, a two-round technical competition designed to test analytical thinking, multitasking ability, and teamwork.

From cracking logic puzzles to building circuits under unpredictable constraints, this event challenges participants to stay sharp under pressure. With problem-solving on one side and strategic gameplay on the other, success depends on communication, adaptability, and technical precision.

Round I: CROSSWORD CONQUEST

In this round, teams take on a timed technical crossword puzzle focused on concepts from Electronics and Communication Engineering. The crossword tests participants’ technical vocabulary, conceptual clarity, and logical connections between topics. Team members must collaborate effectively to decode clues and complete the grid within the stipulated time.

The crossword puzzles are designed with increasing complexity, ensuring that only teams with strong reasoning skills, coordination, and subject understanding advance.

Evaluation Criteria:

  • Accuracy of answers
  • Number of correct entries
  • Time taken to complete the crossword

At the end of the round, the top-performing teams with the highest accuracy and fastest completion time will qualify for Round 2.

Round II: UNO-CONTROLLED CIRCUIT CHALLENGE

Each team consists of two members with distinct roles:

Task Solver

Works on a given electronics problem statement using simulation platforms such as (Wokwi or equivalent tools— yet to be decided ).

UNO Player

Plays an active UNO game against opponents.

The Twist: every UNO card played directly affects the task solver’s ability to work. While the UNO match and technical task run in parallel, the solver must strictly follow the penalties and advantages imposed by the UNO gameplay.

UNO Card Effects

Draw 2
A random task solver freezes (no typing) for 2 minutes.
Draw 4
A random task solver freezes for 4 minutes.
Wild (Colour Change)
Opponent team’s solver loses access to one specific key (e.g., spacebar, backspace, or enter) for 5 minutes.
Reverse
Roles swap: UNO player becomes the task solver and vice versa.
Skip
All task solvers receive a 1-minute timeout.
UNO Call (at 1 card)
Solver earns a Safe Round and becomes immune to the next penalty.
Failure to Call UNO
Team’s solver loses 30 seconds of active coding time.
Crazy 8
Solver gains immunity from all timeouts for a limited duration.

The first team to successfully complete the given technical task wins the round. If no team completes the task, the winner is decided based on maximum progress achieved with the fewest penalties incurred.

RULES

  • Each team must consist of exactly two members.
  • Prior registration is mandatory through the official event portal. On-spot registrations will not be permitted.
  • The decision of the judges is final and binding. Any violation of rules may lead to immediate disqualification.
