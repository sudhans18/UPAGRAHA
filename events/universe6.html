About the Event

Wolf of Tech Street is a high-energy technical showdown that blends analytical thinking, strategic decision-making, and real-world problem-solving. Participants are challenged to decode technical fundamentals, evaluate constraints, and build practical solutions under budget limitations. Inspired by market-style dynamics, the event rewards smart planning, innovation, and confident execution.

ROUND 1: JUMBLED WORDS

THE ELIMINATION PIT

This rapid-fire round tests participants’ familiarity with essential technical terminology and concepts. Teams must quickly decode jumbled words within the given time, emphasizing accuracy and speed.

  • Elimination Round Survival of the fittest. Only the sharpest advance.
  • Technical Core Teams solve jumbled words based on technical fundamentals.
  • The Clock Accuracy and speed determine your final market standing.
  • Final Six Only the top six teams qualify for Round 2.

ROUND 2: THE PROBLEM STATEMENT

BID, BUILD & PITCH

Shortlisted teams face a real-world problem that must be solved through strategic planning and resource allocation. Teams are given a fixed virtual budget and must bid on companies offering relevant technologies to build their solution.

  • The Capital Each team receives a virtual capital of ₹1,00,000. Don't waste it.
  • The Market A list of companies and offerings will be displayed for bidding.
  • Strategy Teams must bid strategically within the allocated budget.
  • Innovation Using acquired resources, teams design a feasible solution.
  • The Pitch Each team must pitch to the panel, highlighting practicality and impact.
