SORCERER REALM

Paper Presentation

About the Event

Sorcerer Realm is a paper presentation event that provides a platform for students and researchers to showcase innovative ideas, research outcomes, and technical solutions. Participants present their work through a structured review and presentation process, encouraging originality, clarity, and technical depth in emerging and impactful domains.

ROUND 1: ABSTRACT SUBMISSION

THE SCREENING

In this preliminary phrase, participants must submit an abstract outlining their idea or research. The abstracts are evaluated for relevance, originality, and clarity. Only the worthy shall pass through the veil to the next round.

  • Initial Casting Participants must submit an abstract for initial screening.
  • The Selection Only shortlisted abstracts will qualify for Round 2.
  • The Scroll Shortlisted teams must submit their full paper before the event day.
  • The Law The paper must strictly follow the IEEE format.

ROUND 2: PAPER PRESENTATION

THE REVELATION

Shortlisted teams will present their work before a panel of judges. The presentation evaluates technical understanding, clarity of explanation, and the ability to answer questions effectively under scrutiny.

  • The Stage Each team is allotted 10 minutes for their presentation.
  • The Inquisition A 3-minute Q&A session follows the presentation.
  • The Verdict Final results are based on the judges' evaluation.
