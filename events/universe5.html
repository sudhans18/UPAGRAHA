About

Event

Sorcerer Realm is a paper presentation event that provides a platform for students and researchers to showcase innovative ideas, research outcomes, and technical solutions. Participants present their work through a structured review and presentation process, encouraging originality, clarity, and technical depth in emerging and impactful domains.

Round I

Abstract Submission & Screening

In this round, participants submit an abstract outlining their idea or research. The abstracts are evaluated for relevance, originality, and clarity. Only shortlisted teams will advance to presentation round and will be required to submit their full paper in prescribed format.

Round I Rules

Abstract & Paper Evaluation

  • Participants must submit an abstract for initial screening.
  • Only shortlisted abstracts qualify for Round 2.
  • Shortlisted teams must submit full paper before event.
  • The paper must strictly follow IEEE format.
Round II

Paper Presentation

Shortlisted teams will present their work before a panel of judges. The presentation evaluates technical understanding, clarity of explanation, and ability to answer questions effectively.

Round II Rules

Presentation & Q&A

  • Each team is allotted 10 minutes for presentation.
  • A 3-minute Q&A session follows presentation.
  • Final results are based on judges' evaluation.
Domains

Focus Areas

  • VLSI Design
  • Machine Learning
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Antenna Design and Applications
  • Signal Processing and Communication
  • Optical Communication
Protocol

Event Rules

  • Teams must consist of 2–3 members.
  • Participants should report 30 minutes before their scheduled slot.
  • Professional conduct is mandatory throughout event.
  • Recording of sessions is prohibited without prior permission.
