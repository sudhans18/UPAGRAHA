ROUND I: THE SIGNAL TOLERANCE TEST

Teams quickly analyze technical scenarios and estimate accurate numerical results. Questions are displayed via PPT, and participants must calculate the answer, write it on paper, and lock a tolerance band within the given time.

  • 90 seconds per question
  • Write answer + lock tolerance band
  • For example, ⚪ Silver (±5%) = 10 PTS
  • Points awarded only if within range
  • Out-of-range = 0 PTS
  • Top teams advance to Round 2

ROUND II: THE RELAY (BID & SOLVE)

This round blends strategic betting with rapid problem-solving. Teams assess topics and difficulty levels, decide how many points to risk, and solve problems under tight time constraints.

8 QUESTIONS Technical and Non-Technical questions from various domains
BID STRATEGY Only topic & difficulty revealed before betting
90 SECONDS Solving window per problem
DOUBLE OR NOTHING Correct = 2x bet | Wrong = Lose bet

>>> FINAL QUESTION: First-to-respond sprint with bonus! <<<

EVENT RULES

  • Teams: 2-3 members
  • Calculators allowed
  • Bets cannot be changed
  • Coordinators' decision is final
