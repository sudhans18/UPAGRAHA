FAST & CURIOUS

FAST & CURIOUS is a two-round technical battlefield where observation, reasoning, and instinct decide survival. Designed for Electronics and Communication Engineering enthusiasts, the event strips away obvious clues and forces teams to read between the signals.

Under relentless time pressure and limited information, participants must analyze visuals, trust their technical memory, and act with confidence. Speed, strategy, and sharp thinking are the only resources that matter.

ROUND I: QUIZ CHALLENGE

A pen-and-paper elimination round testing foundational and advanced concepts of Electronics and Communication Engineering.

  • 20 questions × 1 mark
  • 5 challenge questions × 3 marks
  • Total duration: 30 minutes
  • No negative marking
  • Internet usage strictly prohibited

ROUND II: BID THE REVEAL

Three teams enter a high-stakes reveal-and-bidding arena. Confidence, component knowledge, and reaction speed determine victory.

Reveal 1
Highly blurred image
Reveal 2
Slightly clearer view
Reveal 3
Clear image without labels
Reveal 4
Pins or symbol close-up
Reveal 5
Real-world usage hint

After identification, teams must complete a cup pyramid speed challenge before announcing their answer.

FINAL RULES

  • Teams consist of exactly two members
  • Judges’ decisions are final
  • Only Round II scores decide the winner
