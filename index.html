About UPAGRAHA'26

UPAGRAHA'26 is a national-level technical symposium conducted by the student clubs of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering. Designed as a space for curiosity, creativity, and technical exploration, the symposium invites students to step beyond textbooks and experience learning through challenge and innovation. With a dynamic blend of technical events, presentations, and interactive activities, UPAGRAHA'26 offers an exciting platform to test ideas, collaborate with peers, and dive into the evolving world of technology.

About Us

This symposium is organized by the student associations of the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering - The Electronics and Communication Engineers Association (ECEA) in association with the Institution of Telecommunication Engineers - Students' Forum (IETE-SF, SVCE) and the Research Association for Innovative Design in Communication and Electronics (RACE) - working together to promote innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence. Through symposiums, hackathons, workshops, robotics events, and research-driven initiatives in embedded systems, machine learning, and VLSI, the clubs bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application, empowering students to grow into future-ready engineers and technology leaders.
